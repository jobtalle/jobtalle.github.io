About

My name is Job Talle, and I'm a freelance software engineer, consultant and programming enthusiast. I'm especially interested in researching and developing genetic and evolutionary algorithms. Since 2015, I've been involved in organizing the yearly Global Game Jam in Groningen amongst other activities connecting businesses and academics in the area.

I have broad experience with graphics programming, data visualization and developing simulations. I have studied Artificial Intelligence at the University of Groningen, where I also taught a computer graphics course.

This website was built to write about interesting topics I encounter during my work and studies. The posts are also meant to elaborate further on open source software that I publish regularly on Github.
