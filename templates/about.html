About

My name is Job Talle and I'm a freelance software developer, artificial intelligence student and programming enthusiast. I currently live and work in Groningen, a small city in the Netherlands. I run a company focusing on both game development and artificial intelligence, which are the fields I'm most passionate about. I'm also co founder and treasurer of a local game developers cooperative in Groningen, called the Game Bakery. We have about twenty game related companies as members sharing an office, organizing events and taking on projects together.

I have broad experience with many programming languages and techniques. Some of my favourites are C, C++ and Java, preferably mixed with graphics programming.

I have built this website to write about interesting topics I encounter during my work and studies. The posts are also meant to elaborate further on open source software that I post on Github.
