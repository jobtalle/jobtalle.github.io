About

My name is Job Talle and I'm a freelance software developer, consultant and programming enthusiast. I run a company focusing on software engineering with a specialization in artificial intelligence. I'm also co founder of a game developers cooperative in Groningen called the Game Bakery, where approximately twenty-five game development related companies share an office, organize events and take on projects together. Since 2015, I'm also involved in organizing the yearly Global Game Jam in Groningen.

I have broad experience with many programming languages and technologies, preferably mixed with graphics programming, data visualization and simulations. I have studied Artificial Intelligence at the University of Groningen, where I also taught a computer graphics course.

This website was built to write about interesting topics I encounter during my work and studies. The posts are also meant to elaborate further on open source software that I publish on Github.
